# Feature: Guest interview schedule Phase 2
Status: Draft
Actor: Guest Candidate, HR, HR Staff
Priority: Medium
Related Data: `Interview`, `Application`, `Employee`

## Muc tieu
Quan ly lich va ket qua phong van cua ung vien theo tung application.

## Luong muc tieu
1. HR/HR Staff tao lich phong van cho application.
2. He thong tao record `Interview`.
3. Guest thay lich phong van sap toi trong dashboard.
4. Sau phong van, HR cap nhat `Result` va `Status`.
5. Neu dat, application co the chuyen sang buoc offer.

## Truong hien thi cho Guest
- Ten job.
- Vong phong van.
- Thoi gian.
- Dia diem hoac meeting link.
- Trang thai.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Mot application co the co nhieu interview round.
- [ ] Guest chi thay interview thuoc application cua minh.
- [ ] HR/HR Staff co the cap nhat lich va ket qua.
- [ ] Interview bi huy/doi lich hien thong bao ro rang.
